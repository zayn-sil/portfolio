Case Studies

Organic Viral Growth Strategy

Context

Transformed an underperforming organizational channel with stagnant reach (avg. 1K) into a high-growth platform.

Strategy

Implemented a "Hook-Value-CTA" framework for short-form video. Optimized SEO keywords in captions and leveraged trending audio within the first 48 hours of velocity.

Outcome

266K+ organic views on a single reel, 91% reach from non-followers, and a 400% increase in profile visits.

Bubble.io Community Activation

Context

B2B SaaS platform needed to increase diversity in their accelerator cohort without increasing paid ad spend.

Strategy

Shifted from broadcast messaging to peer-to-peer activation. Facilitated LinkedIn Lives with alumni and activated niche Slack communities.

Outcome

30% increase in qualified applications and strengthened long-term retention in the community ecosystem.

Newsletter Retention Ecosystem

Context

Social following was growing, but owned audience (email) engagement was stagnant at 20% open rate.

Strategy

Aligned email content with social trending topics. A/B tested subject lines for urgency vs. curiosity. Cleaned list hygiene to improve deliverability.

Outcome

Open rates doubled to 41%, subscribers grew by 27%, and the newsletter became a primary driver for event ticket sales.
